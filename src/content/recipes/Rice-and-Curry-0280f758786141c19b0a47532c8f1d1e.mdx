## Okra curry (Ladyfingers)

- 100 grams okra
- 1 teaspoon raw curry powder
- 1/4 teaspoon Tumeric powder
- 1/2 salt
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon onion chopped
- 1 teaspoon Garlic
- 1 teaspoon green chilli
- 1 teaspoon of curry leaves
- 1 cup of coconut milk

Steps: Take a pan and out cut okra and all the other ingredients, add coconut milk too cook until
tender.
