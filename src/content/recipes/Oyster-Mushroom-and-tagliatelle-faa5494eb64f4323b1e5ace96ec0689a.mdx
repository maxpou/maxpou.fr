## Ingredients: (2 persons)

- Pasta (tagliatelle)
- Oyster Mushroom (200g)
- White wine (~1 glass)
- garlic (~4 cloves)
- 10cl cream
- Espelette pepper
- salt, pepper and parsley
- Optional: parmesan cheese
