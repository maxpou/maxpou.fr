## Ingredients: (2 persons)

- ~15 shrimps
- a bunch of cilantro
- 1 clove of garlic
- 2 lime (juice+zest)
- 1 green jalapeño
- 10cl olive oil
- radish and cucumber

## Steps

Green sauce: In a blender, put the jalapeño, cilantro, garlic, lime juice and the olive oil. Blend
until the texture looks smooth. This part can also be done with a hand blender.

Cut the shrimps in half, remove the digestive tract (dark part).

In a plate, spread the sauce and add the shrimp.

Cover with some radish, cucumber, Espelette pepper and lime zest
