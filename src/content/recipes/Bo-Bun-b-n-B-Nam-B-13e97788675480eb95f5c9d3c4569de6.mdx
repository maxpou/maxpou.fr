## Ingredients (2 persons)

- Rice vermicelli
- 3-4 radishes
- 1 carrot
- 1 salad - mint/coriander
- 1 lime
- sesame seeds

## (optional) Meat part:

It needs to marinade for ~6h before cooking

- 300g of beef
- 1 big onion (sliced)
- soy sauce
- Fish sauce (nuoc mam sauce)
- 1 clove of garlic
- grated ginger (1cm)

## Variants:

- 1/4 cucumber diced
- peanuts
