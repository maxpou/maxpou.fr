Rice and Curry

I got this recipe during a cooking lesson in a place called Matey Hut in Ella, Sri Lanka.

All curries are listed from the middle-top, then going clockwise.

Okra curry (Ladyfingers)

  • 100 grams okra
  • 1 teaspoon raw curry powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Tumeric powder
  • 1/2 salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon onion chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic
  • 1 teaspoon green chilli
  • 1 teaspoon of curry leaves
  • 1 cup of coconut milk

Steps: Take a pan and out cut okra and all the other ingredients, add coconut milk too cook until tender.

Eggplant curry

  • 1 big eggplant
  • 1 tablespoon onions
  • 1 teaspoon garlic
  • 1 teaspoon green chilli
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 teaspoon curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon chopped tomato
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon raw curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seed
  • 1 teaspoon roasted curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon roasted chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek
  • 1 teaspoon mustard cream
  • 1 tablets spoon viniger
  • 2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon/cards man powder
  • 2 cups coconut milk

Steps: cut eggplant like French fries & deep fry until golden brown, keep a pan on fire & add coconut oil & all ingredients except tomatoes & tempered. Put coconut milk & make gravy. then add fried eggplant/tomato & gently tossed & get.
