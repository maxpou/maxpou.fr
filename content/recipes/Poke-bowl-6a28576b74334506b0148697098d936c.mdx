## Ingredients: (per person)

- carrot
- 1/2 avocado
- salmon
- mango
- rice (50 - 60g)
- edamame
- sesame seeds

## Salmon **Marination**

1. dice the salmon
2. add the juice of 2 lemons (green)
3. soy sauce

Rest for 30min to 1 hour. If you marinate it for longer, the salmon will start to cook itself in the
marinade.

## Variant

- radish
- purple cabbage
- cucumber
- mango can be replaced with pineapple
- raw cashews
