## Ingredients

- 800g tomatoes
- 200g chickpeas
- Spinaches
- 2 cloves of garlic,
- Curry paste

## Steps

1. in a hot pan, temper the spices
2. add the chopped tomatoes with 100ml of water
3. simmer for ~10 mins
4. add chickpeas and the spinaches
5. simmer for another ~10 mins

Serve with rice
